Discover Liverpool

Liverpool is a vibrant port city in northwest England, famous for its music, football, and waterfront. The city grew around the River Mersey and was once one of the world’s busiest harbours. Today visitors stroll along the Royal Albert Dock, where warehouses now hold museums, shops, and restaurants. Liverpool is also the birthplace of The Beatles, and music fans can explore venues where the band first performed. Sports lovers know the city for Liverpool FC and Anfield’s electric match-day atmosphere. Beyond the big attractions, Liverpool offers friendly neighbourhoods, street art, and a lively nightlife scene. Historic buildings sit beside modern architecture, giving the skyline a unique mix of old and new. Whether you enjoy culture, sport, or simply walking by the water, Liverpool is an exciting place to discover.
